About AI HealthBot

AI HealthBot is an AI-driven public health care assistant designed to provide instant, reliable, and accessible health information to everyone.

Our mission is to help users understand diseases, symptoms, and prevention tips quickly while promoting healthy habits.

This project uses AI to analyze common health questions, offer basic first-aid advice, and suggest when to seek professional medical help.
